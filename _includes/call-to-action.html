Let's keep the world spinning - with us on it

We are working on something really cool, something that's going to be focused on bringing you ethical, sustainable and fair trade fashion apparel, but also tips & tricks on what each of us can do to put the world back on the right tracks.

And no, we're not asking you to give up partying, good music, good food, travel or any other fun and amazing stuff the world has to offer. We want to extend that offer and we want our beautiful planet to keep spinning with us on it.

Movi nun means "move now". We chose the name because we don't want to just stand by, we want to act, do something and help where we can.

So come on, join our community and let's MOVINUN together.
